Overview
Setly is an AI-powered relocation assistant helping students, professionals, and expats
move seamlessly to new cities or countries. From verified housing and community support
to daily essentials, Setly simplifies the chaos of relocation — making 'settling in' feel
effortless.
Value Proposition
“Relocation made human.” Setly combines AI assistance and trusted local communities to
help newcomers feel at home — anywhere in the world. Users get instant access to
housing, essentials, and verified contacts, while partners gain pre-qualified leads for
housing, mobility, and services.
Target Customers
 International Students
 Working Professionals relocating for jobs
 Families moving abroad
 Digital Nomads
Business Model
1. Freemium Model: Basic relocation help for free
2. Premium Plan: Concierge-level assistance & AI automation ($10–$25/month)
3. Partner Commissions: Universities, realtors, travel services
4. Long-term: B2B relocation SaaS for enterprises
