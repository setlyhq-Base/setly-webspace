--- PAGE 2 ---

1. Executive Summary
Setly is an AI-powered relocation and community platform designed to help people move
seamlessly — from international students and professionals to expats and families. We
simplify the process of finding housing, roommates, essentials, transport, and local
connections, all through a single digital assistant.
Our long-term goal: to make moving to a new place feel as easy as opening an app.



--- PAGE 3 ---

2. Vision & Mission
Our mission is to make relocation a stress-free, guided experience through technology and
empathy. Our vision is to become the global platform for relocation and lifestyle transition —
powered by AI and cultural trust.



--- PAGE 4 ---

3. Problem Statement
Millions relocate globally each year for studies, jobs, or lifestyle. Finding verified
accommodation, roommates, essentials, and local guidance is chaotic. No unified platform
currently solves all aspects of relocation — users rely on fragmented apps.



--- PAGE 5 ---

4. Market Analysis & Opportunity
Global Market Size (Relocation & Student Mobility): $35B+ relocation services industry
(2024). 6.5M+ students studying abroad annually (UNESCO). Primary Target: Indian students
moving to the U.S. Secondary: professionals, families, digital nomads.



--- PAGE 6 ---

5. Product & Technology
Core MVP1 Features: AI Relocation Assistant, Verified Housing Finder, Local Connect Hub,
Essentials Marketplace, and Concierge Service. Future Roadmap includes AI behavioral
personalization and integrated partner APIs for insurance, travel, and payments.



--- PAGE 7 ---

6. Business & Revenue Model
Revenue Streams: Freemium community, Premium subscriptions ($10–$25/month), Partner
Commissions, and B2B SaaS Relocation tools for universities and companies.



--- PAGE 8 ---

7. Go-to-Market Strategy
Phase 1 (2025): Launch in the U.S. for Indian students. Phase 2 (2026): Expand to corporate
relocations. Phase 3 (2027): Global rollout to Canada, UK, and Australia.



--- PAGE 9 ---

8. Financial Forecast (3 Years)
2025: Revenue $120K, 10K users. 2026: Revenue $750K, 50K users. 2027: Revenue $3.2M,
200K users. Seed Round Ask: $250K (40% Product, 30% Marketing, 20% Ops, 10% Legal).



--- PAGE 10 ---

9. Vision 2027 – Future Roadmap
Setly will evolve into an AI-driven lifestyle infrastructure for movers worldwide — connecting
housing, payments, essentials, and communities into one adaptive ecosystem.
